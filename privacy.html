Bookbazar.com Privacy Policy

● Bookbazar.com website places special focus on maintaining their customers’ privacy. We collect personal data to enhance customer experience and to improve our products and services. Following is information regarding how we use the collected data:

The data which is collected

● Identity data – this includes your name, language, contact information, location, etc.

● Transaction details – this includes payment or card data, orders and returns, purchase history and details, etc.

● Geolocation data – If you interact with us using your phones, this data is collected.

● Browser information and IP address

What the collected data is used for

● Your data will be used by us primarily for managing your registration, processing your purchases of our products and services, responding to your queries and clearing your doubts, and, if the customer wishes, to send them customized communications.

Data integrity

● Bookbazar collects and processes customers’ personal information in accordance with the privacy policy. We ensure that the data collected and processed by us is used only to provide efficient services to the customer. Therefore, we urge our users to provide accurate information while availing our services.

Security & Confidentiality

● Encryption protocols and software’s are used while transmitting personal information.

● PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) is followed while handling credit card data.
