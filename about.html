Our story begins with you

A Student-to-Student Bookstore!

Bookbazar (owned by 'Sakshi and Kavita') is all about you. Bookbazar is an intercommunity online 'student-to-student' bookstore that connects students to buy or sell used books by making it fast and easy for anyone to post a listing on the web. Sellers can easily earn some extra cash by simply posting used books that are no longer in use for them - thus, giving buyers the opportunity to find great value books at affordable price deals. bookbazar is committed to creating a sustainable and eco-friendly approach to book exchanges. By giving used books a second life, we're reducing waste and promoting a more environmentally conscious student community.

"Selling used second hand books at your own desired price is not an uphill battle anymore. Just sell them on Bookbazar!"
